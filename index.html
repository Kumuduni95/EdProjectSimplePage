Arctic fox

Did You Knew ?

  • An arctic fox's long, fluffy tail acts like a blanket, keeping the fox warm when it wraps the tail around its body to sleep.
  • Arctic foxes can be white, brown, or even blueish-gray. Its color depends on the time of year.
  • An arctic fox follows a polar bear on a hunting trip so it can eat the bear's leftovers.
